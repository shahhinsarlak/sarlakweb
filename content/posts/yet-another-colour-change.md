date: '2026-06-01'

The site has been orange for a while. I wanted something with more colour to it and sage green seemed like a nice place to land.

I used Opus 4.8 through Claude Code to do the swap. The orange accent is gone and everything runs on a sage palette now. The background shifted a little cooler so the green sits right against it. While the files were open I also added a proper light mode and wired up the theme toggle that had been sitting in the header doing nothing.

One colour change turned into a small redesign. That tends to happen.

I keep changing the look because it is my site and I am the one who has to look at it. For now it is green.
